### Hardware
 - Board: [[Sixunited AXB35-02|Hardware/Boards/Sixunited-AXB35]]
 - Backside fan: 120x25 mm
 - Power supply: Huntkey HKA230195A2-0A7
 - Space for NVMe SSDs radiators: at least 73x24.2x10.5 mm (LWH)
